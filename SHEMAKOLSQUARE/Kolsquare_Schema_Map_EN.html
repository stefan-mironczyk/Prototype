          Strategic summary & next steps

          1. Immediate (weeks 1–2): Ship the 6 HIGH priority URLs — starting with /fr and /es homepages — with Organization, WebSite and SoftwareApplication schema to anchor the brand entity for AI citations.
          2. Short-term (weeks 3–6): Apply SoftwareApplication + FAQPage + ContactPage schema to top-conversion MEDIUM pages (TikTok Calculator, Kolculator, Chrome Extension, Demo, Pricing) — the biggest untapped AI-visibility lever.
          3. Mid-term (weeks 7–12): Roll out ItemList + FAQPage schema on listicle pages ("Top 10 Influencers…") — frequently cited as list sources by Perplexity/ChatGPT.
          4. Ongoing: Deploy schema template-based to new pages (Trend = New); refresh declining-high-potential pages and verify their schema.
          Clear recommendation: Start with /fr homepage as the pilot (top traffic + conversions). Then roll out in parallel across the calculator tools (fr/kolculator, fr/tiktok-calculator, en/tiktok-calculator) where conversion potential without schema is still largely untapped.
